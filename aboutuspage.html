About Uganda Martyrs University

Uganda Martyrs University (UMU) is a private, faith-based institution established in 1993. The university is committed to producing highly skilled graduates who demonstrate integrity, professionalism, and excellence in all fields.

  • High academic standards
  • Strong moral and ethical foundation
  • Wide range of academic programs
  • Environment that supports learning and innovation
